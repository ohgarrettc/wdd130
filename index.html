North Carolina

North Carolina, one of the original 13 colonies gained its statehood in November 1789. After succeeding from the Union and joining the Confederate States of America in 1861, it rejoined the Union on July 4, 1868 at the conclusion of the American Civil War. Located in the southeastern region of the United States. It is the 28th largest and 9th most populous. Bordered by Virgina to the north, Tennessee to the west, and touching Georgia and South Carolina on its south, North Carolina has a vast coast that stretches along the Atlantic Ocean. Charolotte, the states' largest city is a hub for tourism, banking, and technology. The Wright Brothers famously made the first ever sustained flight of an aircraft at Kitty Hawk in Outer Banks, NC.
